Welcome to the Mother City

“Tour with Zester” Zester Tour Guide presents to you Cape Town known as the mother city of the country. Cape Town was founded in 1652 when Jan van Riebeeck of the Dutch East India Company based in The Netherlands arrived to set up a halfway point for ships travelling to the East. One of the biggest tourist attractions and best places to unwind and do some shopping for the country’s residents. Furthermore, a place to “rock” for those that love to party. The nighttime partying in Cape town is immaculate and will make one feel very ecstatic, they called the Ibiza of Africa.
